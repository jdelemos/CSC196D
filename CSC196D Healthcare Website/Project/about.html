Get Tokens

Learn how to register and access your government-issued Healthcare Stable Coins (HSC) for medical services.

How It Works

Citizens who are eligible for public healthcare support can receive annual or emergency Healthcare Stable Coin (HSC) tokens directly from the government. These tokens are securely stored in a digital wallet linked to your registered identity.

You can use your HSC tokens at any approved hospital, clinic, pharmacy, or healthcare provider to pay for medical checkups, surgical procedures, medications, and other essential services.

To get started, simply register through our secure platform. Once verified, your tokens will be automatically allocated to your account. Click the "Read More" button below to begin the registration process and start using your healthcare tokens today.

Why Choose Blockchain

Immutable Records

Once data is written to the blockchain, it cannot be altered or deleted. This ensures that all medical expenses, service claims, and token transactions are permanently and securely recorded without risk of manipulation.

Transparency and Trust

Every transaction is publicly verifiable and traceable by both the patient and the healthcare provider. This level of visibility reduces fraud and builds trust in the use of government-issued healthcare funds.

Decentralized Access

With no central server to compromise, patient records and financial data remain secure. Authorized providers and patients maintain shared, tamper-proof access to the information they need.

Streamlined Administration

Automated transaction validation and smart contract execution eliminate redundant paperwork and accelerate approval for care and payments, ensuring timely access to medical services.
